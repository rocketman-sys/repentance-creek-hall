Events

Pizza nights & community events

Pizza nights run on monthly Saturday evenings from 4pm until sold out — a relaxed community evening that's been going since 2012. Dates and the full menu are announced on our Facebook page. Check our upcoming events for the next date.
No booking needed — just turn up. Pizzas are served for a suggested donation (around $22 for favourites like Greek Chicken or Capricciosa). Beer and wine are available at the event, or you can BYO. Indoor seating is available when weather turns wet. Please leave dogs at home.

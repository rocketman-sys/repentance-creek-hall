## 2. About / Description (Official Bio)

Repentance Creek Hall has been serving the community since 1937. The hall is a social and cultural facility for the use and benefit of local community. During disasters it is used as a community hub for communication and provide support.

## 9. Building Description (from photos)

- Heritage green and yellow weatherboard construction (repainted 2026; previously green and white)
- Raised on timber/concrete stumps (Queensland-style)
- Ramp and steps to main entrance
- Surrounded by dense subtropical bushland / rainforest
- Large grassed area in front
- Covered outdoor verandah/lean-to used for pizza prep
- Wood-fired dome pizza oven
- Long timber preparation/serving tables
- Indoor seating available
